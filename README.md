# WindThing
A small yard art wind vain with LEDs that indicate wind speed.

Powered by an 18650 battery and solar charging setup, 10 LEDs on the back of this wind vain will light up as the wind speed increases.
The blades and nose were borrowed from here and modified for this project.  All other 3d printed items were designed by me.
The motor was chosen because of it's low spin resistance and ability to make voltage at low RPMs.
The circuit is designed to be adjustable to your wind conditions using the POT.
